SiLens

An AR application prototype

The core of SiLens

The information we perceive from the outside world is booming and overwhelming. We do wish some of them could be filtered out, and that's when SiLens comes in.


What is SiLens

SiLens is a free app you can install onto your AR lens, and it grants you the right to deny seeing someone.


Usage of SiLens

Add a few members to your blocklist, and SiLens will instantly block them, based on image recognition.
